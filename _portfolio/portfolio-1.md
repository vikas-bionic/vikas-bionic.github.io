This project focuses on overcoming the scalability limitations of wild-type plastic-degrading enzymes for industrial recycling applications. My work primarily targets **Leaf-Branch Compost Cutinase (LCC)** and **MHETase**.

### Key Approaches:
* **Computational Modeling:** Performing structural analysis, molecular docking, and extensive molecular dynamics (MD) simulations using the **Schrödinger Suite** to identify rigidity pain points and predict stabilizing mutations.
* **Experimental Validation:** Translating *in silico* designs to the bench via molecular cloning, expression in *E. coli* systems, and high-throughput enzyme activity assays.
* **Biophysical Characterization:** Assessing structural integrity and thermal melting shifts using Circular Dichroism (CD) spectroscopy, Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC), and fast protein liquid chromatography (FPLC/Gel Filtration).
* **Next-Gen Pipelines:** Bridging active industry collaborations to develop data-driven, AI-powered protein engineering pipelines for accelerated enzyme optimization.
