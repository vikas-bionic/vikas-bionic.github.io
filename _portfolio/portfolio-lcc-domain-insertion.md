Explored the insertion of a foreign domain into Leaf-Branch Compost Cutinase (LCC) to sterically block unwanted LCC-LCC self-association, which can otherwise interfere with the enzyme's catalytic efficiency.

### Key Approaches:
* **Structural Design:** Identifying suitable insertion sites via structural modeling to minimize disruption of the native fold and active site.
* **Molecular Cloning:** Constructing and expressing the domain-inserted LCC variant in *E. coli*.
* **Functional Characterization:** Assessing self-association behavior and catalytic activity relative to wild-type LCC.
