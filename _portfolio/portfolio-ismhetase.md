Rationally engineering IsMHETase, a key plastic-degrading enzyme, to improve its thermostability — a major bottleneck for its use in industrial PET-recycling processes.

### Key Approaches:
* **Computational Modeling:** Structural analysis and molecular dynamics (MD) simulations using the Schrödinger Suite to identify destabilizing regions and predict stabilizing mutations.
* **Experimental Validation:** Molecular cloning, expression in *E. coli*, and enzyme activity assays to test designed variants.
* **Biophysical Characterization:** Assessing thermal stability shifts via Circular Dichroism (CD) spectroscopy.
