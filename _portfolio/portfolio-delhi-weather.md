As a summer intern with the Atmospheric Chemistry and Emissions Research Group at IISER Mohali, conducted a seasonal analysis of Delhi's 2022 meteorological data to identify unique weather events.

### Key Approaches:
* **Instrumentation:** Worked with gas analyzers, performed instrument calibrations, and conducted QA/QC of chemical data.
* **Data Analysis:** Used MATLAB and Igor Pro to process and analyze meteorological time-series data.
* **Domain Learning:** Built foundational understanding of atmospheric chemistry to contextualize seasonal weather patterns.
