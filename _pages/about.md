I am a dedicated researcher and a final-year BS-MS Dual Degree student majoring in Biology (with a Data Science Minor) at the **Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Mohali**. Working at the intersection of molecular biology, biophysics, and computational biology, I apply an interdisciplinary approach to engineer functional proteins for environmental and industrial challenges.

Currently, my research focuses on the **rational engineering of plastic-degrading enzymes** (such as LCC and MHETase) to improve their stability and catalytic activity. My work integrates computational tools like molecular dynamics (MD) simulations with experimental validation, alongside building industry collaborations for AI-driven enzyme design pipelines.

Alongside my research, I have a strong academic foundation, having secured top national ranks including **All India Rank 21** in the CSIR-UGC NET (99.95th percentile) and **All India Rank 75** in GATE Life Sciences.
