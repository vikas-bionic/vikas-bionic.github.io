Welcome to my academic portfolio! I am Vikas, a BS-MS Dual Graduate student with major in Biology and a minor in Data Science from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Mohali.
Working at the intersection of molecular biology, biophysics, and computational biology, I apply an interdisciplinary approach to engineer functional proteins for environmental and industrial challenges.

Currently, my research focuses on the **rational engineering of plastic-degrading enzymes** (such as LCC and MHETase) to improve their stability and catalytic activity. My work integrates application of computational tools such as molecular dynamics (MD) simulations to rationally hypothesis and perform experiments to validate results. Alongside I am also bridging an industry collaborations for AI-driven enzyme engineering pipelines.

Please feel free to explore my CV to learn more about my background, research skills, and qualifications. Let's connect if you want to discuss any ideas.
